Understand the Challenge

Knowledge is our first line of defense. Learn about the impact of drug abuse on individuals and communities.

🇮🇳

Drug Abuse in India

Millions of lives are impacted by illicit drug use nationwide, affecting health, productivity, and the future potential of youth.

🌴

Drug Abuse in Kerala

Outreach campaigns are vital in Kerala's campuses to prevent substance abuse, support peer guidelines, and encourage healthy living.

🎓

Effects on Students

Substance dependency disrupts academic performance, mental stability, interpersonal relationships, and professional prospects.

🏠

Effects on Families

Families carry severe emotional, psychological, and financial burdens when members struggle with addiction issues.

🧠

Mental & Physical Health

Addiction triggers serious health consequences, including cognitive impairment, anxiety, depression, and physical deterioration.

⚖️

Crime and Society

Drug networks fuel local crime, disrupt neighborhood safety, and drain community healthcare and law enforcement resources.

Our Shared Responsibility

Building a drug-free community requires coordinated action between the administration, society, and our youth.

01

Government & Community Duty

Strengthening campus enforcement, developing accessible rehabilitation facilities, and organizing large-scale public safety initiatives.

02

Youth Leadership & Peer Support

Creating supportive environments in classrooms, checking on classmates, and serving as advocates for positive lifestyles.

03

Individual Action

Taking the anti-drug pledge, spreading awareness to at least 3 friends, and helping connect those in need to professional medical advice.
